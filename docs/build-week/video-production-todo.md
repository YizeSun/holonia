## Deliverable constraints

- Maximum three minutes.
- Audible narration.
- English narration or an English translation.

## Planned capture approach

- Use a physical iPhone for the final proof because local-network permission, Bonjour discovery, Keychain-backed device identity, pairing, and real-device installation are part of the claim. A simulator may be used only for supplemental clean UI shots and must be labeled as such.
- Record iPhone and Mac separately at native resolution, then edit them into a synchronized side-by-side layout. This keeps both state machines readable and avoids relying on a camera pointed at the devices.
- Capture one short establishing shot showing both physical devices on the same Wi-Fi if needed to make the setup credible.
- Preserve the original captures. Use the execution receipt and structured diagnostics to align the two timelines.
